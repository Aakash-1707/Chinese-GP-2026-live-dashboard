Our Story

We are a dedicated supplier of high-quality resin raw materials, specially focused on the needs of artists and creators. Our mission is to provide reliable, premium materials that help bring creative ideas to life.

We supply carefully selected resin materials suitable for resin art, jewelry making, coating, molds, and other creative applications. Our products are chosen for their clarity, durability, and consistent performance so artists can achieve the best results in every project.

With a commitment to quality, fair pricing, and dependable supply, we aim to support artists, small businesses, and craft enthusiasts with the materials they need to create beautiful resin artwork.

We are proud to be a unit of Preserving Memories — a brand built on the belief that every creation tells a story worth keeping.
